# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 16 December 2025

## Introduction
Trediq (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates a WhatsApp-based communication service using the WhatsApp Business Platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when users interact with us through WhatsApp.

By communicating with us via WhatsApp, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## Information We Collect
We may collect the following information when you interact with us on WhatsApp:

- Phone number  
- Message content exchanged with our WhatsApp Business account  
- Message metadata such as timestamps and delivery status  

We do **not** collect sensitive personal data unless explicitly shared by the user.

---

## How We Use Information
We use the collected information solely to:

- Communicate with users via WhatsApp  
- Provide customer support and service-related notifications  
- Respond to user inquiries and requests  
- Improve our communication and services  

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

---

## Data Sharing
We do not share personal information with third parties except:

- When required by law  
- When necessary to operate WhatsApp messaging services via Meta Platforms, Inc.  

All WhatsApp communications are governed by WhatsApp’s own privacy and security policies.

---

## Data Storage and Security
We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Messages are stored only for operational and compliance purposes and are retained for a limited duration.

---

## User Consent
By initiating a conversation or responding to our WhatsApp messages, users provide consent to receive messages from us.

Users may opt out at any time by requesting us to stop communication.

---

## User Rights
Users have the right to:

- Request access to their data  
- Request correction or deletion of their data
